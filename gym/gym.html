BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat that helps assess an individual's overall health based on their height and weight. It's calculated using the formula: BMI = Weight (kg) / [Height (m)]². According to BMI classifications, a value between 18.5 and 24.9 indicates a normal weight.

You can calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) by entering your height in centimeters (cm) and your weight in kilograms (kg).
